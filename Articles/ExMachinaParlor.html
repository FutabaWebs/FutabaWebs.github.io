What/Who is Ex Machina Parlor? 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

Now, what is Ex Machina Parlor and what is their goal?


Ever seen the Hackerspace in "Watch Dogs 2" where Marcus, Wrench, and various members of the team work on all sorts of projects, sharing and collabing resources? What if I told you we could have something like that!


What if I told you they could make all of that ACCESSIBLE and AFFORDABLE to you much like with HTB and TCM’s stuff? Do you still follow?! GOOD! Keep reading, it only gets better, PROMISE!


Part of Ex Machina's goal is to create a hackerspace where you can visit either PHYSICALLY or VIRTUALLY, and participate in their overall Cyber range, allowing you to grow as Hackers and Cyber Security professionals.


Essentially Ex Machina's goal is to turn the big TWO, HTB and TCM Security into the big THREE. There's no telling what's possible if they actually manage to pull this off? Not only will you be able to learn the basics from HTB and TCM, BUT, when you’re ready to begin APPLYING the knowledge, you’ll have Ex Machina to help you step up your game!


Imagine stuff like SEIM, SOAR, MITRE setups, even azure active directory stuff you'd probably need to purchase a subscription and a lot more, along with a ton of other stuff all accessible to you now! Imagine wanting to learn how to code, or get HANDS on training with Robotics, stuff you'd HAVE to spend A LOT of money on in order to even do the thing, also available to you now?! This also goes for PHYSICAL hacking tools and a lot more! Imagine all that being accessible to you in one or more PHYSICAL spaces! This is essentially what Hacker culture SHOULD feel like and is what they’re going for!


Oh, did I mention folks there are MEGA friendly and you’ll get CLOSE up guidance from them? That’s mentorship basically!


Wanna help them succeed? Well, their non-profit, so in other words, contribute what you can in order to ensure you as well as everyone else that needs PRACTICAL, both HANDS on as well as VIRTUAL training. Give them a follow and join their discord server to see what their all about!


Link to their discord server can be found here!

The main goal of FREE resources/training is so folks can bypass the paywall barrier, allowing them to have just as much as a chance compared to those holding a 4 year degree along with certs. But, if they aren't even given a chance to apply that knowledge then it's just as useless as a person with a degree/certs, whom might I add, most likely put themselves in debt just to achieve that.
