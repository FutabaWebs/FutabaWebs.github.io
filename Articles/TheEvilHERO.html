What is a Gray Hat Hacker? 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

Gray Hat Hackers are a mixture of quality traits between White Hat Hackers, whom are good guys, and Black Hat Hackers, whom are bad guys, incorporating a series of ethical and unethical means in order to achieve their objective which tends to be more along the lines of “Hacktivism” for a greater cause or purpose. To put it into better perspective, they are GOOD guys that aren’t afraid to get their hands dirty, hence being in the gray area swaying either way. Their skill sets also tend to be a bit broad while having a certain niche.

A PERFECT example of one would be the infamous “Anonymous” group, where they target a lot of terrorist groups and corrupt organizations and expose them for the betterment of the world! They do whatever it takes to get the job done incorporating a wide variety of techniques, which allows them to achieve feats the ordinary Hacker/Cyber Security professional might have never thought were possible.

Be mindful, although Gray Hats aren’t afraid to get their hands dirty and will do ANYTHING to get the job done, they don’t usually resort to killing, as they tend to be more on the pacifist side, not ALL, but most in general.

You can find out more about the various "Hats" in this article I wrote here.

Hunter Hunter 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

I’ve talked about this before in my “The One Above All” article, but one of the main reasons why hackers do what they do is because THEY LOVE the THRILL of the hunt. What it is they like to hunt depends on the hacker which will greatly influence the skill sets they possess.

For example, CTF players that hack on a comptetional level, love the overall thrill of finding the exploit to secure the flag which tests the full limits of their capabilities. It’s exhilarating knowing you’ve pwned a system and have TOTAL control over it, as well as the many secrets it possesses. It’s even more exhilarating if you apply it on an unethical level and dabble in what you shouldn’t (I’m NOT encouraging you to do this!)

In this case, since we’re talking about Gray Hats, the thrill of the hunt for us is hunting whatever it is we’re most passionate about when it comes to activism.
