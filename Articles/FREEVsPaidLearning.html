🎃 FREE Vs PAID Learning, Which is Better?

What is FREE learning? 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

FREE learning is just that, FREE! It requires ZERO money in order to learn. The only thing that IS required: is a computer, home internet, and the DISCIPLINE to learn on your OWN, emphasis on that last part! Technically you have to PAY in order to get these things, but I digress.

FREE learning is just another term for “Self-Taught”, which is common nowadays! A lot of people, when getting into Web Development was the new trend, were often commonly Self-Taught, myself included when I first started learning how to code. There were an ABUNDANCE of resources available online so it only made sense to teach yourself. Learning to code was no longer gatekeeped knowledge, so LITERALLY, ANYONE could learn how to code for FREE!

This is convenient for those that are unable to afford a FORMAL education from an institution with DIRECT guidance, where they can essentially learn all the SAME material on their own! This increases accessibility across the board!

HOWEVER, it did create one fatal drawback, which I’ll discuss later on over the course of this article.

Should FREE learning exist? 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

YES, it ABSOLUTELY should exist. Knowledge should be FREE! There’s a reason PUBLIC libraries exist! It’s also WHY the internet exists, so we can share said FREE information with each other, helping everyone in the long run!

Anyone should be able to pick up a book or a computer and have the ability to learn on their own and at their own pace! It’s a human right!

How to Go About FREE Learning? 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

The best way to go about it is to get into the habit of filtering through information.

In case you haven’t noticed this about me by now… I AM GOOD at filtering through what I NEED to know Vs what I DO NOT need to know.

A lot of people struggle with this, even experienced professionals, but if you can get GOOD at this, then you’ll progress rather quickly.

The DrawBacks of FREE Learning? 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

I’ve talked about the BENEFITS of FREE learning, but now let’s dive into the DRAWBACKS of it!

For starters…. YOU ARE ON YOUR OWN! You only have access to what you can find in order to learn, which there is PLENTY of!

“But Futaba if that’s the case, then why is that a DRAWBACK?!?! There are PLENTY of FREE material online for me to learn from so I-”

OBJECTION!!!

I think you haven’t been paying CLOSE attention to when I’ve stated “ON YOUR OWN”!” That’s right…. YOU ARE ON YOUR OWN, as in SOLO, NO HELP from ANYONE! No one’s holding your hand so you LITERALLY have to sit down and FIGURE IT OUT on your OWN!

THIS!!! is the BIGGEST drawback to FREE learning, ESPECIALLY, when it involves Cyber Security which is HUGE in and of itself!!!!!!

When I first started learning about Cyber Security on my OWN, I had NO IDEA what I was doing or where I was going. NO ONE pointed out ANYTHING as to where I needed to go career wise. I had to sit down and figure it out! Even with ALL the FREE resources out there I was still running around with my head chopped off not knowing what the hell I was doing for YEARS!!

WHY? Well, let me elaborate with this next point!




Mentorship is NOT FREE! 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

Although FREE learning should exist, it does NOT mean however that FREE guidance should exist. You can’t have your cake and eat it too!

The price you pay for FREE learning is NO FREE MENTORSHIP or guidance.

“BUT THAT’S NOT FAIR THE KNOWLEDGE SHOULD BE FREE!”

Here’s the thing…. IT IS FREE!!! It’s EVERYWHERE!!! HOWEVER, what is NOT FREE, is expecting FREE labor AKA mentoring/tutoring from people. You have to be mindful, mentoring IS A FORM OF LABOR, labor which is NOT FREE. There are people out there that give back to the community which is nice, but you’re NOT entitled to it!

This might rub A LOT of people the WRONG way, but ask yourself this question, would YOU do it for FREE?

When Should You Have to Pay? 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

It’s a no brainer on this one, but, like I’ve stated, when you’re looking for direct guidance from someone, OR, if you’re of course looking to acquire TOOLS/SOFTWARE to enhance your learning experience.

Also… as I’ve stated, there are MANY niche sources of knowledge, or QUALITY material, like from HackTheBox or TCM for example, where the only way to acquire it is through PAID methods. No one’s going to hand you what they purchased for FREE so don’t even think it works like that! I want their research content as well, but sadly, even I know in order for me to acquire it I would HAVE to pay for it.

Going back to the tools/software thing, yes, physical tools like the wifi-pineapple, AREN’T FREE. You have to PAY for them! Even things I’ve mentioned earlier: PC gear, internet, etc, all these things, in order for you to START learning for FREE, REQUIRE that you PAY for them FIRST!

Advanced IDS systems that you can use to build homelabs, oftentimes some of them are both hardware and software, which require you to PAY, hence most people can’t get hands-on experience with A LOT of this stuff unless they work for a company that has it.
