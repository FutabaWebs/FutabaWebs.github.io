What is A.I.? 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

A.I., which stands for Artificial Intelligence, is the overall main replication of human intelligence that implements what is known as DSA, Data Structure Algorithms, whose goal is to create an archive collective of data that we can essentially train into a program so that it can make critical decisions for us based off of the information stored in it. Think of it like raising a child, and slowly educating it over the years. The human brain has the learning capacity to retain experiences far and wise no matter how much time has passed, which is what we are trying to simulate. This is also what is commonly known as Machine Learning. One of the most commonly used programming languages for data structures is poses and brags PYTHON.
