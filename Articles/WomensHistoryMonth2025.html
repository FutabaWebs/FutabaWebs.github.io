Using this information, Jennifer is able to run security assessments and test for some of the most critical ones or latest ones against any business model she secures, checking if it exists, mitigating it should it exist!


She’s also known as “Alt-Funktion”, and runs her own YouTube channel and content that breaks down a lot of CTI and other Cyber Security subject matter in a meaningful and engaging way, easy for folks to understand. She even has her own blog which you can find here!
