🎃 The Beginning of CTOS?

It’s CTOS 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

This thing is LITERALLY the next evolution of potential smart city technology, where EVERYTHING is connected: phones, cars, even CCTV surveillance security systems for homes which is shown in the video for it.

If you’ve been paying CLOSE attention, despite it advertising itself as a “mobile” OS platform, it’s heading in the overall direction of smart city technology where you can LITERALLY unlock and remotely control your motor vehicle and a lot more with it. It’s CONVENIENT…. TOO CONVENIENT!!!

The direction it’s going overall is for EVERYONE to integrate and use it in their daily lives, which is an easy pitch seeing its overall convenience!

Although the advertisement for it claims it has security in mind, this still doesn’t take away from the HUGE risks that come with it, the BIGGEST risk being that EVERYTHING IS CONNECTED!

We need to be mindful… EVERYTHING IS HACKABLE! Many people hear security will be in place for something nowadays…. and then it inevitably gets hacked!

Everything is Connected! 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

One of the biggest gamblers when it comes to security risks is anything that is of monolithic structure, where everything is condensed into one place.

Seeing as this is going in the overall direction of everything being connected, one can assume it’s also going in the overall direction of a central network that also connects everything tied to it. Even if it doesn’t, there’s still a risk for anyone that is using it, in that they’ll have more vulnerable networks where a lot more is on the line should it be compromised.

Nowadays when threat actors breach a network, they usually have to work for their food and pivot in order to hit everything. With Harmony OS, we’ve essentially decreased the amount of work they have to do, in that all they need to do is hit ONE weak spot, such as your mobile device for example, and they’ll be able to compromise and control everything!

It’s BellWeather 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

One can only assume this thing is going the direction it does to up and funnel data which is a high form of currency nowadays. Most places VIOLATE your privacy and have been CAUGHT selling your data!

They’ll be able to learn EVERYTHING about you simply from you using this system. In other words… THEY’LL BE ABLE TO MONITOR YOUR EVERY MOVE on a more scary level!




It’s LETHAL 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

Remember that “it can control your car thing” I was speaking about before? Imagine what would happen if someone HACKED it!

Wanna hear another scenario even more frightening? Imagine there was a sauna room that was connected using Harmony OS? Hackers could LITERALLY trap you in there and rig it to COOK you alive if they wanted to!

With everything connected it’s easy to rig various systems to HARM people if need be. This is one of the MAIN reasons why anything along the lines of smart city technology is usually taboo!

Can You Use It? 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

We have to accept that technology is going to evolve and advance to levels like this. You can still use it…. BUT…. BE SMART while doing so.

The danger with using Harmony OS is interconnecting everything you own, so… simply DON’T do that. Everything in moderation basically!
