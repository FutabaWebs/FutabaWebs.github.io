They ARE the ORIGIN 🕸🕸🎃🕸🕸

I’ve talked about this before… but WOMEN ARE THE TRUE FOUNDERS for anything related to computing technology.

Stuff like: The first human readable programming language such as COBOL which made programming more accessible to everyone, WI-FI which we all use today, Bluetooth, even the source code for the Apollo space project which brought us to the moon for the first time, were all created by women, most of which were NEVER properly credited to them. Even the first digitization of the “computer”, based on the analytical engine by Ada Lovelace, wouldn’t have been possible without them.

“But there are videos and history texts and-”

Yeah, but most of it is thrown under the rug without much coverage! Women were ROBBED of the spotlight they deserved, where the impression was given that MEN did it first.

They were known as “Refrigerator Ladies” where they were never really given credit for all the hard work they did.

You can find out in more detail about their major contributions in tech in this article here! I wrote for Women’s History month.
